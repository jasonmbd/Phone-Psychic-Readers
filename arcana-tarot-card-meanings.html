What Is the Arcana in Tarot?

"Arcana" means secrets or mysteries. A tarot deck of 78 cards is built from two arcana. The Major Arcana — 22 cards, also called Trump Cards — represents the major situations and turning points everyone faces, the big forces shaping the long-term direction of a life. The Minor Arcana — 56 cards across four suits — fills in the day-to-day details: the people, choices, and influences around your question. Read together, the two arcana tell the whole story.

Major Arcana vs. Minor Arcana

When the Major Arcana dominates a spread, the reading is about the big picture — major lessons and fated turning points. When the Minor Arcana leads, it's about the everyday particulars you can influence. Most readings mix the two, and a skilled reader weaves them into one story.
