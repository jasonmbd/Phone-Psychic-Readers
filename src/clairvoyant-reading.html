What Is a Clairvoyant?

A clairvoyant is an individual with the ability to gain information about a person, event, object, or location through extra-sensory perception. The term comes from the French clair ("clear") and voyance ("vision"). A clairvoyant can see images in their mind that relate to you, pick up situations from the past, give insight into the present, and see what is coming. A reading helps you better understand who you are and where your life is heading.

What Is a Clairvoyant Reading?

Most readers are both psychic and clairvoyant, so it doesn't matter exactly how the reading is referred to. Your clairvoyant may draw on clairaudience (hearing relevant messages), clairsentience (linking into feelings), and sometimes clairolfaction (sensing familiar smells) — using all of these to give you the clearest insight possible.

What Are the Different Types of Clairvoyants?

Clairvoyance takes many forms. Here are the main ones our readers practice:

  • Augury

    Insight drawn from acute, precise observation of the natural world, interpreted by experienced readers.

    Clairvoyance in space and time (psychometry) — using an object as a reference point to surface images and emotion.

  • Astral Clairvoyance

    Perceiving beings with no physical body — requiring high focus and care from the practitioner.

  • Spiritual Clairvoyance

    Focused on virtue, spirituality, and consciousness, working within established natural law.
