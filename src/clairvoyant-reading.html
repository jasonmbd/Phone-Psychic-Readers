What Is a Clairvoyant?

A clairvoyant is an individual with the ability to gain information about a person, physical event, object, or location through a form of extra-sensory perception. The term is derived from the French clair, meaning "clear," and voyance, meaning "vision." A clairvoyant can see images in their mind that relate to a person and pick up situations from the past. They also give insight into present circumstances and see what is coming up in the future. When you get a reading from a clairvoyant, it can help you better understand who you are and where your life is heading.

What Are the Different Types of Clairvoyants?

Palmistry

A form of fortune-telling that uses the lines or creases on an individual's hands to provide a reading on life, career forecasts, and other future events. A palm reader usually begins by asking to see your dominant palm. Each crease or line is associated with areas such as your romantic life, background, or work; some readers also focus on cuticles, fingernails, and other natural marks.

Tarot Card Readers

One of the most recognized forms of clairvoyance. The tarot deck is divided into two groups, the Major Arcana and the Minor Arcana; cards are drawn and laid out in a symbolic order. A professional tarot card reader often spends months or years honing their skills and gaining a full understanding of each card's meaning.

Augury

A type of clairvoyance that depends on acute, precise observation of the natural world for insight. Augury requires interpretation by trained and experienced individuals well versed in reading visions and signs.

Spatial Clairvoyance

Known as clairvoyance in space and time. Psychometry leverages an object as a starting and reference point. Some people have no idea they possess any psychic ability until they touch antiques or old objects and vivid images and emotions surface.

Astral Clairvoyance

The ability to perceive living beings that have no physical or tangible body. This ability requires high levels of focus and care, as the practitioner may contact beings of many types.

Spiritual Clairvoyance

This ability focuses on allegiance, virtue, and true spirituality, found within the purview of our spiritual awareness. All life and consciousness ultimately comes from the Divine, where everything manifests under established natural law.

Is a Clairvoyant Different From a Psychic?

A psychic is someone with heightened intuition and other skills — a "sixth sense" used to pick up and communicate information about your past, present, and future. A clairvoyant is someone who has developed the psychic ability to see images or pictures in their mind's eye to add detail within the reading. Clairvoyants can read over the phone because they use your voice vibration coupled with the advanced intuition they have developed over the years. Becoming a clairvoyant is a gift acquired throughout a psychic's life and is often present from birth.

Clairvoyant Abilities

  • Clairvoyance — the ability to see clearly. Auras, spirits, and the dynamics between people can all be unraveled in a clairvoyant reading.
  • Clairaudience — clear hearing. The ability to listen to sounds, communication, and feedback from spirit guides, and to notice vibrations that are otherwise inaudible.
  • Clairsentience — clear feeling, sometimes called clear knowing. Similar to gut instinct or intuition, but elevated to a much higher level.

Types of Clairvoyant Readings

Clairvoyant readings are very useful when you need clarity about a current situation — your job and career, relationships, love life, health, or general direction.

General Life Direction and Purpose

There are periods when you don't have a specific problem but are instead looking for your life's direction or purpose. Clairvoyant readings can be exceptionally helpful here, providing a wide view of your present circumstances and what brought you to this point.

Relationships

A common reason to call for a clairvoyant love reading is advice about love and relationships — it's hard to see clearly when your emotions are involved. A clairvoyant usually can't tell you whether someone is in love with you, but they can give you information about the direction a relationship is heading and what may be influencing your decisions. A good clairvoyant won't tell you what to do; they give you information about your situation and the other person so you can make your own decisions.

Health

Some clairvoyants specialize in readings about health and well-being and can gain information about the state of your body by psychic means. These readings should be a supplement to care by a licensed physician, not a replacement. Many clairvoyants will not give a health reading unless you are already under the care of a doctor.

Career

One of the top reasons people seek clairvoyant readings is to make decisions about their career — finding a path, weighing a job offer, or deciding whether to stay or move on. A trustworthy clairvoyant can see your current situation and give you insight, such as what the company you're considering may be like or how likely a new opportunity is.
