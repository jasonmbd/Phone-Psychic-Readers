What Is an Annual Horoscope Reading?

An annual horoscope reading interprets the year ahead using the transits and progressions moving across your personal birth chart — not a generic Sun-sign column. Your astrologer looks at where the slow, important planets are travelling through your chart over the next twelve months, the major themes that puts in motion, and the windows where things tend to open or tighten. It's a year-ahead map built from your own wiring, designed to help you plan instead of just react.

How Is This Different From the Yearly Horoscope I Read Online?

Night and day. The yearly horoscope you read online is written for an entire Sun sign — the same forecast for everyone born across a whole month, often by a writer who never sees your chart. An annual horoscope reading is calculated from your exact date, time, and place of birth, so the timing and themes are specific to you. Two people with the same Sun sign can have completely different years; this reading reflects yours, not the average of millions.
