The Sun Card — What It Shows

The Sun card represents optimism, fulfillment, and positivity. A large sun shines in the sky, the source of life on our planet. A young child plays joyfully, showing the innocence and happiness that comes from being true to yourself. The child sits on a white horse — the joy of being connected to your inner spirit, with the horse a symbol of purity and strength. The child is naked, showing there is nothing to hide or be ashamed of.

The Sun Upright Meaning

Upright, The Sun is one of the brightest cards in the deck — success, joy, vitality, and clarity. It tells you to have confidence in your spirituality and to avoid the cultural constraints that keep you from being yourself. When The Sun shines on a question, it favors warmth, openness, and things going well by simply being authentic.

The Sun Reversed Meaning

Reversed, The Sun tells you to humbly count your blessings and to give credit to those who helped bring your success about. The light is still there; the reminder is gratitude and humility rather than taking the warmth for granted.

The Sun as Advice

As advice, The Sun counsels confident authenticity: stop dimming yourself to fit, trust your own light, and let success come from being openly who you are.
