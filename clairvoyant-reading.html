What Is a Clairvoyant?

A clairvoyant is an individual with the ability to gain information about a person, event, object, or location through extra-sensory perception. The term comes from the French clair ("clear") and voyance ("vision"). A clairvoyant can see images in their mind that relate to you, pick up situations from the past, give insight into the present, and see what is coming. A reading helps you better understand who you are and where your life is heading.

What Is a Clairvoyant Reading?

Most readers are both psychic and clairvoyant, so it doesn't matter exactly how the reading is referred to. A clairvoyant reading by phone combines a mixture of gifts so your reader builds a strong connection with you. It usually starts with your clairvoyant listening to your voice vibration while tuning in to their spirit guides. Tarot cards may also be drawn for more detail. Your clairvoyant may draw on clairaudience (hearing relevant messages), clairsentience (linking into feelings), and sometimes clairolfaction (sensing familiar smells) — using all of these to give you the clearest insight possible.

Clairvoyant Reading — Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a clairvoyant, a psychic, and a medium?

All mediums are psychic, but not all psychics are mediums. A psychic tunes into the energy and vibrations around you to read your past, present, and future. A clairvoyant is a psychic who specifically sees — images, colors, symbols, and pictures in their mind's eye ("clear seeing"). A medium is a psychic who connects with loved ones who have passed. Many of our readers work in more than one of these ways.

How does a clairvoyant reading work over the phone?

Clairvoyance isn't limited by distance. Your clairvoyant tunes into your voice vibration and energy to receive intuitive impressions, images, and messages — no need to see you. Phone readings are based on energy, not appearance, which is why they work just as well as in person.

Are clairvoyant readings accurate?

No psychic is 100% accurate every time — accuracy is influenced by your openness and the clarity of the connection. Honest, experienced clairvoyants are often highly accurate, but the real value is the insight, validation, and clarity a reading gives you so you can make better decisions, not a guaranteed prediction.

Can a clairvoyant see the future?

A clairvoyant sees the patterns and energy at work in your life and where things are likely to head if nothing changes. They often see the present clearly and show you likely outcomes — but your choices still shape what actually happens. It's guidance, not a fixed fate.

Can a clairvoyant contact a loved one who has passed?

That's mediumship. Some of our clairvoyants are also mediums and can connect with loved ones on the other side — just tell our team that's what you're looking for and we'll match you with a reader who specializes in it.

What questions should I ask a clairvoyant?

Open-ended questions get the most from a clairvoyant. Instead of "Will my ex come back?" try "What energy is around this relationship, and how can I move forward?" Save simple yes/no questions for the end — open questions give your reader room for the depth and nuance they're best at.

How do I prepare for a clairvoyant reading?

Set a clear intention for what you want clarity on, find a quiet space where you won't be interrupted, take a few deep breaths, and keep a notebook handy. Stay open-minded — readings often include symbols or visions that make full sense a little later.

What can a clairvoyant tell me about?

Love and relationships, career and money, life direction and purpose, recurring patterns, spiritual guidance, and well-being. A clairvoyant describes what they see about your past, present, or future to help you see your situation clearly.
