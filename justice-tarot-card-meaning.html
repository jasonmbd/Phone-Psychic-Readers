The Justice Card - What It Shows

Justice is a symbol of fairness, truth, and law. A woman sits on a chair holding a sword in her right hand and a weighing scale in her left. The sword symbolizes impartiality; the scale shows how she listens and balances logic. A square crown on her head symbolizes the clarity required to dispense justice. Beneath her red cloak, the tip of a white shoe appears - a spiritual reminder that the rulings she makes are according to the outcomes of someone's actions.

Justice Upright Meaning

Upright, Justice is accountability and truth coming into balance. As advice, it tells you to listen carefully to people's accounts of themselves so you can make a wise and accurate decision. This is the card of cause and effect: outcomes are being weighed honestly, and what you've put out is part of what comes back.

Justice Reversed Meaning

Reversed, Justice means a deeper meaning is being withheld for a reason only a higher power understands. The full picture isn't available to you yet - judgment may need to wait until more truth surfaces. It cautions against deciding firmly on incomplete information.

Justice as Advice

As advice, Justice asks for honesty and fair hearing: weigh all sides, own your part in cause and effect, and make the decision the facts support - not the one your bias prefers.
