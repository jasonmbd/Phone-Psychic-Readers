Phone Psychic Readings — Frequently Asked Questions

How do phone psychic readings work — do they need to see me?

No. A phone psychic doesn't need to see you. Psychic insight isn't limited by distance — your reader tunes into your energy through your voice and intention. When the call starts, your psychic greets you, explains how they work (tarot, astrology, or pure intuition), and then tunes in to your question. You can call from anywhere, in private.

Are phone psychic readings accurate?

Yes. Many of our readers find phone readings just as accurate — sometimes more so — than in person. Without visual distractions, your psychic focuses entirely on your voice and energy. A reading shows the patterns at work in your life and the likely direction things are heading, not a fixed, unchangeable future.

How much does a phone psychic reading cost?

First-time callers pay just $1 per minute. You can also bundle 15 minutes for $10. Returning callers pay the per-minute rate of their chosen phone psychic — and you can hang up anytime.

How do I know your phone psychics are real and not a scam?

Every phone psychic on our line is hand-vetted by our master psychics — only a small percentage of applicants are accepted. We will never ask for your Social Security number, bank details, or passwords, and our readers never use fear tactics or claim you're "cursed." A genuine reading only needs your name and your question.

What information should I never share during a phone psychic reading?

Never give any psychic service your Social Security number, bank or card details, or account passwords — a real psychic never needs them. We only need your first name and the question you'd like guidance on. Be cautious of anyone pressuring you to keep a reading secret or to pay off-platform.

How do I prepare for a phone psychic reading?

Find a quiet space where you can speak freely, clear your mind, and write down one or two specific questions beforehand. Stay open to the answers you receive. It helps to take notes during the call — details are easy to forget afterward.

What questions should I ask a phone psychic?

Open-ended questions get the most useful insight. Instead of "Will I get back together with my ex?" try "What energy is influencing this relationship, and how can I navigate it?" The more focused your question, the clearer the guidance — but avoid oversharing every detail upfront so your reader can tune in cleanly.

What can a phone psychic tell me?

Our phone psychics give guidance on love and relationships, career and money, life direction, emotional confusion, recurring patterns, and your path ahead. They read the momentum in your life and likely outcomes — they won't hand you a fixed fate, they help you see clearly and decide with confidence.

If your time runs out you can choose to continue or hang up. If you continue, it's simply at the per-minute rate of your phone psychic. As you approach zero minutes you'll hear a prompt so you're never surprised.
